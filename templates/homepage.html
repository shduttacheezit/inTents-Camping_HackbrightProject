WELCOME TO INTENTS LENDING



Camping expands your mind. The adventurer in us thrives when we spend time outdoors.

The problem? Camping gear is expensive! And heavy, ooof. But, did you know that, most often than not, gear just sits in at home, in a closet, unused? This is why I've built inTents Lending; to connect campers to lenders who are willing to lend their gear, using convenient pickup and drop off locations based on your geolocation.

With a commitment to sustainability and the outdoors, inTents Lending wants everyone to seek out the adventure that's out there!
